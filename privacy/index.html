Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information RankWebs uses when you visit the site. Last updated: June 11, 2026.

Information we store in your browser

RankWebs may save your selected language in your browser's local storage so the site can open the same language next time. This preference stays on your device and can be cleared through your browser settings.

Server and hosting logs

The site is hosted as a static website. Hosting providers may process standard technical logs such as requested URLs, browser type, approximate time, referrer, and IP address for security, reliability, abuse prevention, and diagnostics.

Cookies, analytics, and advertising partners

RankWebs does not require account cookies to use the directory. We may use analytics and advertising services to understand site usage and support the site.

Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Users can also control cookies through browser settings or visit aboutads.info to learn about choices offered by participating third-party vendors.

Search queries and outbound links

Search happens in the browser against the website ranking data loaded by the page. When you click a listed domain, you leave RankWebs and visit an external website. External websites have their own privacy policies and content practices.
