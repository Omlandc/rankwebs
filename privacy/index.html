Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information RankWebs uses when you visit the site. Last updated: June 10, 2026.

Information we store in your browser

RankWebs may save your selected language in your browser's local storage so the site can open the same language next time. This preference stays on your device and can be cleared through your browser settings.

Server and hosting logs

The site is hosted as a static website. Hosting providers may process standard technical logs such as requested URLs, browser type, approximate time, referrer, and IP address for security, reliability, abuse prevention, and diagnostics.

Cookies and third-party services

RankWebs does not require account cookies to use the directory. Some third-party services may use cookies or similar technologies when they are loaded by the page. These technologies are controlled by the relevant third party and your browser settings.

You can control cookies through your browser settings and review privacy options offered by your browser or any third-party service you use.

Search queries and outbound links

Search happens in the browser against the website ranking data loaded by the page. When you click a listed domain, you leave RankWebs and visit an external website. External websites have their own privacy policies and content practices.
